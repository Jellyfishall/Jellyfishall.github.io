Fault-Tolerant Synchronization for Remote Pseudo-Random Functions

  • Devised a PRF synchronization method via code-number compression.
  • Simulated & analyzed performance in Python; failure ≈ 10−6 at channel loss 0.05.
  • Co-inventor on patent for short numbering embedding synchronization.
