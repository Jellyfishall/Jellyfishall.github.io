I am a PhD student of the RAINZ-CDT programme at The University of Manchester. My research interest includes Human-Robot Interaction, Cognitive Robotics and Affective Computing. Specificly, my recent research project focuses on Theory of Mind and Intention Reading in Human-Robot Interaction. I received my MRes degree from The University of Edinburgh, researching on Affective Computing of Human-Robot Interaction.
